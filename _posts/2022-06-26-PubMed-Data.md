##  Data

Data is colelcted from the Pubmed Medline, Arxiv and Bioarxiv journals for neuroscience papers published for the past 5 years.
The fields colelcted are:
- `PMID`
- `Title`
- `Abstract`
- `List of Authors`
- `Affiliation`
- `source` (Medline | arxiv | bioarxiv)
- `Publish year`
